How to read T12 baseline waste in this chart:
Start with the difference between supplied kVA and consumed kW across the month. The visible green reactive layer is non-productive current that still loads the transformer and conductors.

For T12 in January, modeled electrical waste is 151,101 kWh for the month, and peak reactive demand reaches 1,088.7 kVAR. That waste appears as avoidable thermal and current burden rather than useful process output.

When this gap grows, you are paying to transport and cool inefficiency. The chart helps you see where and when that burden is most concentrated.
