Cherry Avenue Facility Overview

The Cherry Avenue facility is one of Foster Farms’ monitored operating sites in California’s Central Valley. Unity Energy has been studying this site since May 2024 to observe electrical loading, field efficiency, thermal burden, and voltage behavior across its four primary 480V transformer fields. Refered to as : T10, T12, T15 and T16

This page serves as the entry point to Cherry Avenue’s measured energy architecture.
