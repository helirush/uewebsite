Unity Energy analyzes how electricity actually behaves inside your facility—not just how much energy you consume, but how efficiently your electrical infrastructure delivers power to production.

In this transformer, we identified elevated reactive circulation and harmonic instability. These conditions increase electrical current, generate unnecessary heat, and consume transformer capacity without increasing output.

Using your measured operating data, Unity determines where your system truly operates during production and designs a correction matched to that real demand.

For this facility, Unity recommends installation of two MPTS systems to stabilize the electrical environment and reduce circulating reactive energy.

After installation, power factor rises to near unity, thermal stress across equipment declines, and previously unused electrical capacity becomes available for productive load.

The projected result is approximately one hundred sixty thousand dollars in annual operational savings with an expected payback period of about two and a half years.

If you'd like, Unity can now walk you through the full analysis showing exactly how these results are achieved.
